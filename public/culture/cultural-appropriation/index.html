What is cultural appropriation?

Cultural appropriation is broadly defined as the use of a culture‚Äôs elements by a member of another culture. According to scholars, cultural appropriation covers much more than a list of easily recognisable symbols, artifacts or rituals.

What is culture?

Culture is not defined by ethnologists and folklorists as a solid and stationary unit.
